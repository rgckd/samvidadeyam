Sharing Knowledge for Spiritual Growth

“Samvida Deyam” is a phrase derived from the Taittiriya Upanishad, which is a part of the Vedas. It means to give with understanding, implying that the giver should be aware of the situation, the recipient, and the appropriateness of the act.
